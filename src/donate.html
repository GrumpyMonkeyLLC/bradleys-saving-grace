Where Your Money Goes

Your Support Makes This Possible

Every dollar goes directly toward reuniting dogs with their families. Here's how your financial support helps:

🚁

Drone Search Operations

Funding our sponsored thermal drone operators who can cover vast search areas quickly — day or night — dramatically increasing the chances of a successful recovery.

🪤

Trapping & Recovery Experts

Supporting professional trapping and recovery specialists who have the skills and equipment to safely capture frightened or injured dogs who won't come when called.

📋

Flyers, Signs & Printing

Covering the cost of printed flyers, yard signs, and banners that blanket the search area with your dog's photo and information — because visibility saves lives.

🤝

Community Resources

Purchasing equipment, supplies, and resources needed to connect lost and stolen pets with their owners across our community and beyond.

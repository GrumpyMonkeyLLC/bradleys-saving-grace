How It Started

Born from heartbreak,
built with purpose

We are missing our boy so much. After the painful outcome of our own situation, we made a promise — we do not want others to go through what we went through.

That's why we are forming a nonprofit in Bradley's name. Our Facebook page is now called "Bradley's Hugs" and is slowly becoming a resource hub for families facing the nightmare of a lost or stolen dog.

Please be patient with us as we go through the needed steps to get this all in motion. We are working steadily, and every step is taken with love for Bradley and for every dog still waiting to come home.

What We Stand For

Our Mission

To provide resources and support to reunite lost and stolen dogs with their families through rapid response, community engagement, and advanced tracking and recovery solutions — while advocating for stronger protections to keep pets safe and promote awareness on the local, statewide, and national levels.
⚡

Rapid Response

Every hour matters. We're building systems for faster, coordinated community alerts and action.

🤝

Community Power

Neighbors helping neighbors. We expand search networks and keep eyes on the ground where it matters.

📢

Advocacy

Pushing for stronger legal protections at local, state, and national levels so fewer families face this pain.

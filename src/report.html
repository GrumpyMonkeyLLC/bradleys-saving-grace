⚠️ Dog Stolen? Act Fast

File a police report immediately. Get a case number — you'll need it. Contact local animal shelters and document everything.

🚀 Immediate Steps

  • Post on local Nextdoor, Facebook groups
  • Call all shelters within 50 miles
  • Walk the neighborhood calling their name
  • Put worn clothing outside — scent attracts them
  • Contact your vet to flag the microchip
  • Post flyers within a 1-mile radius
  • Register on PawBoost, Finding Rover

Our community volunteers may be able to assist with searching, flyer distribution, or guidance.
